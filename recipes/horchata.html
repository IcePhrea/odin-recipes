Easy Horchata



Description

Horchata is a delish treat to enjoy any time of the year. This recipe is easy to make and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Rice
  • Sugar
  • Milk

Instructions

  1. Soak Rice, Water and Cinnamon
  2. Blend after 24 hours
  3. Strain mixture
  4. Add sugar, milk and mix
  5. Enjoy
