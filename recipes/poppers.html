Jalepeno Poppers

Ingredients

  • Cream Cheese
  • Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • Bacon Bits
  • Jalepeno Peppers
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  • De-seed peppers
  • Pack with cream cheese and bacon bits
  • Bread peppers
  • Fry for 2-3 minutes, or until golden brown
